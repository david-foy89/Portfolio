About Me

Full stack developer building real products with modern web tools

I'm David Foy — full stack developer, Tennessee-based, always in the middle of building something.

Coding started as curiosity. Now it's the thing I do when I want to solve a problem, when I want to make something real, and honestly just when I'm bored. I care about the details: clean logic, solid APIs, and interfaces that feel right to use.

I work across the full stack with React, TypeScript, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. I've shipped a full-stack forum with JWT auth and real user flows, and a browser-based tool suite for a game community that people actually use every day.

Looking for a role where good work is noticed and fast growth is expected. Let's build something.
